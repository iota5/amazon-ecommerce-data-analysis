# Amazon E-Commerce Data Analysis (SQL + Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end analysis of an e-commerce dataset containing over **113,000 orders** using **MySQL and Power BI**.  

The objective of the project was to analyze revenue performance, product behavior, customer segments, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction in order to generate actionable business insights.

The dataset provided by Newton School contains two main tables:

• Orders  
• Customers  

The analysis includes SQL exploration, Power BI dashboards, DAX calculations, and business recommendations.

## Key Business Problems Solved

### Revenue Analysis
- Analyzed revenue trends by year and product
- Identified top-performing and low-performing products
- Calculated product revenue contribution and ranking

### Returns Analysis
- Calculated total returned products and return rate
- Identified main return reasons such as defective items and delivery errors
- Analyzed category-wise return patterns

### Customer Behavior
- Verified whether product ratings impact sales
- Designed a loyalty program using RM segmentation (Recency–Monetary)

### Geographic Performance
- Analyzed revenue contribution across zones and locations
- Identified high-performing regions such as Greater Accra and Ashanti

### Sales Optimization
- Identified months with lower sales performance
- Recommended targeted promotional strategies

### Product Performance
- Identified low-performing products requiring marketing attention
- Determined products suitable for targeted discounts

### Logistics & Operations
- Analyzed delivery type vs waiting time
- Studied average wait time across product categories
- Examined relationship between shipping charges and product type

### Customer Satisfaction
- Investigated factors influencing low product ratings
- Proposed strategies to reduce customer dissatisfaction

---

## Key Insights

• Revenue remained stable between **2015–2019**, followed by strong growth in **2020**.  
• Revenue is highly concentrated among a small number of products and categories such as **Electronics and Phones & Tablets**.  
• The dataset shows a **27% return rate**, mainly due to defective products and logistics errors.  
• Product ratings show **minimal correlation with revenue**, indicating other factors influence purchasing decisions.  
• Revenue contribution is geographically concentrated in **Zone 3** and key locations like **Greater Accra and Ashanti**.  
• A large portion of revenue comes from inactive customers, highlighting opportunities for **customer re-engagement strategies**.

## Business Recommendations

• Strengthen product quality control to reduce return rates.  
• Improve logistics accuracy to prevent missing or incorrect deliveries.  
• Introduce targeted promotional campaigns during low-sales months (June and September).  
• Bundle low-selling products with high-performing items to increase visibility.  
• Implement a structured loyalty program to improve customer retention.
